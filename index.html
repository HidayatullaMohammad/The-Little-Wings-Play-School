Our Philosophy & Story

At The Little Wings, we believe every child is born with unique wings of talent. Our mission is to give them a safe sky to test those wings and soar.

A Nest Crafted with Love

Established with the vision of offering high-standard preschooling in Vuyyuru, The Little Wings Play School integrates modern early-childhood methodologies with emotional care. We place safety, sanitation, and child happiness at the center of everything we do.

Our campus on AG & SG College Road features bright, beautifully themed classrooms, certified child-friendly play equipment, and constant security surveillance to assure parents of absolute safety.

Simple Admissions Journey

Our admission process is transparent, stress-free, and designed to help you and your child feel completely comfortable.

1. Digital Query

Fill out our short online contact form or call us to share basic details about your toddler.

2. Campus Walkthrough

Visit our school at Kota Complex, AG & SG College Road. Witness our safety system and colorful wings firsthand.

3. Play Consultation

A friendly chat and observation session where our developmental counselors help determine the right wing level for your kid.

4. Spread Wings!

Complete basic documentation (birth certificate, immunization cards) and receive your child's welcome backpack kit!
